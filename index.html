How to Sell Gold in the UK

Selling gold can be a good way to turn unwanted jewellery, scrap gold, or coins into cash. The amount you receive usually depends on three main factors: weight, purity, and the current gold price.

Before selling, it helps to know whether your gold is 9ct, 14ct, 18ct, or 22ct, because higher purity usually means higher value.

What Affects Gold Value?

  • Weight in grams
  • Gold purity
  • Live market gold price
  • Buyer margin and service quality
